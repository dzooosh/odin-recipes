Ewa Agoyin

Description

The Ewa agoyin is an african which can be eaten either as fast food or cooked at home for family and friends. It can be eaten with other food substances like Cassava flakes or bread or eaten ordinarily

Ingredients

  • Beans (Black eyed peas)
  • Dried chili
  • Red Ball Pepper
  • Onion
  • Palm Oil
  • Seasoning and spices

Steps

  1. Pick out the dirts in the beans and boil water
  2. Pour the picked beans in the boiling water and add Onion
  3. Put down when beans is super soft
  4. Make the the agoyin stew by cooking up palm oil and adding all necessary ingredients.
  5. Put either fish, crayfish to give to a wonderful taste.
